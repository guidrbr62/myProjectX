- colored 20 page booklet
- digitally remastered

Tracks 1-10 = Gamut Sessions (1985)
Tracks 11-19 = Aircraft Sessions (1987), should have been their debut full-length album, supposed to be released by PARC Records, then Jet Screamer but never realized. These recordings were spread as "Jet Screamer" demo.

Recording information:

Tracks 1-10 recorded end of 1985 at Gamut Productions.
Tracks 11-19 recorded in winter of 1987/1988 at Aircraft Recording Studios, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA.
Remastered at The Basement Recording.
